Examining a role for S100A12 in vascular inflammation

I worked with a team to demonstrate for the first time that O antigen, a ubiquitous bacterial polysaccharide, prevented infection by bacteriophage T4.

Our findings were published in the Journal of Experimental Microbiology & Immunology+ (JEMI+), UBC's undergraduate research journal.
